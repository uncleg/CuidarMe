Who are we?

CuidarMe! is a contact network where parents interested in the welfare of their children meet each other with professionals in the care of them.

In addition, parents and professional can access reputation, availability and interests of the members of the network. In this way we build together a network of trust towards our children.

How we can help you?

We have extensive contact base of qualified babysitters.

You can access not just references but also know their studies, availability and taste.

This way you can find the babysitter that best suits your needs and those of your children.

School help you need for your children can find in Cuidarme!.

You can access the expertise, references and availability of school helpers for your children. This way, your choice will be much easier.

If you have trouble finding a partner for your home with good references in Cuidarme! we can help.

In our broad base of contacts you'll find that person of confidence you need to perform the chores you need.
